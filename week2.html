Wiggam Week 2: CAD, Fusion 360 and Design

In this section we learned to use Fusion 360. We started out getting used to the challenging software and then took on our own project. For my project I made a headphone stand to put on my desk along with the CSGO logo engraved on the top. Narrative: This project taught me how to maneuver in Fusion 360. I started by created ractangles and then piecing them together in the shape of my headphone stand. I then extruded the item to give it depth and to make it 3D. I used the chamber and fillet tools to round off my edges and to make the item look more friendly and contemporary.
